Travel Kit

  • 1/Phone with a good battery + power bank etc.

  • 2/Stock up on medications for adults and children (antibiotics)

  • 3/Go to the dentist and take care of your teeth first, make a visit to your doctor if necessary.

  • 4/Diapers for your baby, take as many as you can.

  • 5/Don't overburden yourself with winter jackets, one jacket per person.

  • 6/Don't forget your medical records (chronic illnesses, interventions, etc.)

  • 7/All your documents uploaded to IRCC.

  • 8/White background ID photos, 8 pieces each.

  • 9/For electronic devices: don't bring hair dryers or irons as the voltage is different. For phone and laptop chargers, you can buy plug adapters once in Canada.
