Once you arrive at Canada's YUL airport

1/

Register your passports at the self-service kiosks.

2/

Activate the Wi-Fi on your phone.

3/

Follow the arrows and read the signs to go to the immigration officers' desk. Take a ticket and wait (this may take a few hours, so be patient!).

4/

Present your passports, permanent residency confirmation documents, proof of funds (bank statement), and the address where you will be staying (either Airbnb, family or friends, or a personal rental).

5/

Collect your luggage and head to the airport exit.

6/

Once outside, if you don't have transportation for your luggage or to your destination, check our offers and availability on the website. We will provide transportation at competitive prices.
