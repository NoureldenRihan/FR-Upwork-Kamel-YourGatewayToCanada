Arrival Kit

  • 1/ Purchase a SIM card. Chatr: $32.00/month, 50 GB of data, autopay bonus, unlimited calls across Canada on 2G network.

  • 2/ Obtain a SIN (Social Insurance Number): Visit the nearest SERVICE CANADA with your passport and permanent residence confirmation document. Take a ticket and wait for your turn.

  • 3/ Obtain a Permanent Resident card: Contact IRCC and follow the instructions, especially for address change if necessary, so that your card is sent to your current address.

  • 4/ Obtain a Health Card: Visit the nearest SERVICE ONTARIO with your passport, permanent residence confirmation document, lease contract, and the completed and signed form (available on-site). Take a ticket and wait for your turn. After registration, expect to receive your card by mail within 1 to 2 weeks.

  • 5/ Open a bank account: We will help you choose the bank that suits you. Bring identification (passport or permanent resident card), the permanent residence confirmation document, the lease contract, and the SIN document.

  • 6/ Register for Ontario Works: Create an online account at https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-works. Follow the steps: download and fill out the forms, send the requested documents, wait for the results. Once accepted, follow your programs on the site or via the MY BENEFITS app (Android or iOS).

  • 7/ Register at the YMCA: https://www.ymca.ca - 1 - Make an appointment for an assessment of your English level to study or improve your English for free (part-time or full-time). 2 â€“ Request to be referred for the furniture bank. 3 - Actively search for a job in collaboration with Ontario Works.

  • 8/ Apply for the Canada Child Benefit: Download the forms at https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/child-family-benefits/canada-child-benefit-overview/canada-child-benefit-how-apply.html#how. Complete them, attach the required documents, and send everything to the indicated address. Wait 8 to 11 weeks to receive the funds.

  • 9/ After acceptance by Ontario Works: Request the "starter kit" containing basic household items.

  • 10/ Purchase the OC Transpo card: https://www.octranspo.com/en/fares/payment/presto-en/ With the help of Ontario Works, you only pay 42% of the amount for one month of public transportation throughout Ontario.

  • 11/ Visit the furniture bank: To get good-condition used furniture (sofa, table, chairs, wardrobe, etc.) for $200 to $250, including transportation.

  • 12/ Register at the food bank: For food assistance and baby diapers if needed. Available once or twice a month depending on eligibility.

  • 13/ Enroll in the Ontario Electricity Support Program: https://www.ontarioelectricitysupport.ca/ Financial assistance up to $83 per electricity bill for 24 months.

  • 14/ Register for a family doctor: https://www.ontario.ca/page/find-family-doctor-nurse-practitioner

  • 15/ For Algerian immigrants: Create an account at https://capacitepc.interieur.gov.dz for: 1 - Driving license capacity 2 - Consular card

  • 16/ Obtain a driver's license: Make an appointment on Drivetest.ca. With capacity: G1 then full G license within 1 month max. Without capacity: G1 in 8 months, then G2 in 24 months for the full G license. You should also go to PAF (Francophone Welcome Point) + the nearest community center.
